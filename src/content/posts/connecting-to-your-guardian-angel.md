## Understanding Guardian Angels

A guardian angel is a being of pure love, assigned to you from birth to offer guidance, protection, and support throughout your life. They exist to help you experience love, joy, and reach your full potential, remaining with you through every challenge and triumph.

### Angels in History

The idea of angels has appeared in many cultures and religions:

- **Zoroastrianism:** Amesha Spentas act as celestial guides representing divine wisdom.
- **Judaism:** Malachim serve as messengers and protectors.
- **Christianity:** Angels appear in hierarchies, including Seraphim, Cherubim, and Archangels.
- **Islam:** Angels record deeds, deliver revelations, and protect humans.
- **Ancient Greece and Rome:** Daemons and Genius guide and protect individuals.
- **Egypt:** The Ka and Horus acted as spiritual protectors.

Throughout history, angels have been seen as intermediaries between the divine and human, offering guidance and protection.

### What Is a Guardian Angel?

Your guardian angel knows you intimately, offering unconditional love and support. Most people cannot see their angel, but they can hear, feel, or sense their presence. Connection requires trust and openness, allowing love to flow and building a unique bond.

### How Angels Communicate

Angels communicate differently for everyone. They may appear as:

- Light, colours, or energy
- Sounds, music, or gentle nudges
- Feathers or symbolic objects
- Full angelic figures

Angels can express masculine or feminine energy depending on the connection and situation, transcending human gender entirely.

### Forming a Bond

The bond with your angel is formed through shared frequencies of love, often before your birth. Your angel understands your essence completely, and this connection can never be broken. God works through your angel to guide and protect you throughout your life.

### The Experience of Bonding

Feeling your guardian angel's love can resemble the joy and excitement of falling in love, though it is not romantic. It is a reunion with a soul that has always loved you, offering peace, contentment, and a deep sense of being understood.
