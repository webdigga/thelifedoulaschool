Your intuitive and psychic gifts are inherent abilities that lie dormant within you, waiting to be uncovered. These gifts can come to the surface once you have peeled back the karmic layers of negative behaviour and wounds from all your past lives. These layers act as barriers, preventing your natural abilities and divine gifts from rising to the surface.

## Healing and Making Peace with Your Past

The first step in uncovering your intuitive gifts is to heal and make peace with your past. This involves acknowledging and addressing the negative behaviours and wounds that have accumulated over your past lives. By doing so, you can release the karmic baggage that is holding you back and create space for your natural abilities to emerge.

## Raising Your Vibration

Once you have begun the healing process, the next step is to raise your vibration to that of the universal energy of creation and love. This higher vibration will align you with the energy of the universe, allowing you to access your intuitive gifts more easily. Practices such as meditation, gratitude, mindfulness and energy healing can help you raise your vibration and connect with the universal energy.

## Accessing the Akashic Records

As you heal and raise your vibration, you will be able to access the Akashic Records energetically. The Akashic Records are a metaphysical concept that refers to a compendium of all universal events, thoughts, words, emotions and intent ever to have occurred in the past, present or future. They are often described as a library or database that holds the collective wisdom and knowledge from all your past lives. They contain information about everyone you have ever been, as well as the gifts and abilities from those incarnations. By accessing the Akashic Records, you can tap into this wealth of knowledge and uncover your intuitive gifts.

## Embracing Your Gifts

Finally, once you have uncovered your intuitive gifts, it is important to embrace and nurture them. Trust in your abilities and allow them to guide you on your journey. Remember that these gifts are a natural part of who you are, and by embracing them, you can live a more fulfilling and authentic life.

## A Journey of Healing and Growth

Uncovering your intuitive gifts is a journey of healing, self-discovery and growth. By peeling back the karmic layers, raising your vibration and accessing the Akashic Records, you can unlock your natural abilities and embrace the divine gifts that are within you.

This work of healing the karmic barriers so your natural gifts can rise sits at the heart of the wider teaching; you can explore it further through [the 8 Bridging Keys](/8-bridging-keys/).
