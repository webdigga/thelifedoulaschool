## Understanding Universal Energy

Universal energy is a profound concept where everything in the universe is made of energy and interconnected. From the pen in your hand to the planets, stars and galaxies beyond, everything is part of this vast energetic web. Your power to create possibilities extends far beyond the limits of the human self.

Within you lies an ageless, timeless soul, connected to everything that ever was, is, and will be. By tapping into this universal energy, you can accelerate the healing of physical and emotional wounds.

## Techniques to Harness Energy

Harnessing universal energy involves quieting your mind, opening your heart, and allowing the energy to flow through you. Practices include:

- **Meditation**: focus and calm the mind to receive and direct energy.
- **Visualisation**: imagine energy flowing through and around you for healing.
- **Energy healing practices**: techniques like Reiki connect you with universal energy to promote balance and wellbeing.

Raising your energetic vibration aligns you with the frequency of the universe, helping you draw more positive energy into your life.

## The Science Behind Energy and Magnets

Energy is not limited to living things or the present moment. For example, a pencil made of wood still contains the energy and memories of the tree it came from.

Magnets and energy are intrinsically linked through electromagnetism. Key points include:

- **Electromagnetic fields**: magnets produce magnetic fields, influencing other particles and materials.
- **Quantum mechanics**: electron spin creates tiny magnetic fields, which combine to form larger fields in materials.
- **Human energy fields**: since humans are made of electrons and atoms, we also generate and respond to energy fields. Raising your energetic vibration allows you to co-create change and attract positive experiences.
