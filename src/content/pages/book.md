_Birthing Joy ~ Releasing Pain_ is Becca's book, and it is also a course: a written companion that holds your hand through a healing journey, one cycle at a time. It carries the subtitle _Orphaned to Cherished, Hopeless to Held, Secrets Revealed_, and it grew out of her earlier book, _Birthing the New You, Releasing the Old_, which has been rewritten from the ground up as the teaching deepened. What started as one book is becoming three, and it is still being written. Nothing here is for sale; this page simply shares the work as it becomes ready.

> Let this book hold your hand and walk you back home to your true self.

## Cycle One: Acceptance

The journey begins with Acceptance. In Becca's words:

> The purpose of acceptance is to allow yourself permission to slow down and stop, reassess your life, so that you can shift your direction and focus back to your destined path and prepare you to receive what is truly yours.

Every lesson within Acceptance is broken down into three parts: a **Morning Intention**, a simple Angel prayer setting the intention for the day ahead; a **Daytime Focus** of easy-to-follow exercises for work or home life; and an **Evening Contemplation** that replaces screen time with thought-provoking reflection before sleep. It is a school workbook style of teaching, built for anyone who is low in confidence or new to self-help, with lessons that link into a buildable course: The Open Door, Leaving the Mind Behind, The Fight of Your Life, and onward.
