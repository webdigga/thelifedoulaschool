This site is built to leave you alone.

## What this site collects

Nothing. This is a fully static website: it sets no cookies, runs no analytics, includes no tracking scripts, and has no forms. Visiting it sends nothing about you to me.

## Hosting

The site is served by Cloudflare Pages. Like any web host, Cloudflare processes technical connection data (such as IP addresses) to deliver pages and protect the service. That processing is governed by Cloudflare's own privacy policy and is not accessible to, or stored by, The Life Doula School.

## Embedded videos

Some pages can play YouTube videos. No YouTube content loads until you choose to press play on a video; if you do, YouTube (Google) may set cookies and collect data under its own privacy policy. Videos are embedded via YouTube's privacy-enhanced player to keep that to a minimum.

## Email

If you email me, I will have your email address and whatever you choose to write. It is used only to reply to you, never shared, and never added to any list.

## Changes

If the site ever starts collecting anything, this page will say so plainly before it happens.
