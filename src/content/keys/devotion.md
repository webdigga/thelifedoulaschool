---

Devotion is love with a routine. Not grand gestures or public displays, but the quiet decision to return, day after day, to what matters: your practice, your people, your purpose, your connection with the unseen. This key teaches that steadiness is a form of love, and that small things done faithfully carry more weight than large things done once.

Devotion is never performance and never obligation. It does not push, promote, judge, or force. It simply keeps showing up, and in time the showing up becomes the bridge.
