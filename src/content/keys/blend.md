Blend is the key of the middle path. It refuses to choose between heaven and earth, because a whole life needs both: the washing up and the wonder, the budget and the blessing. This key teaches how to fold spiritual practice into ordinary days and how to let ordinary days become spiritual practice, until the seam between them disappears.

When Blend is working, nothing about your life needs to look outwardly different, yet everything is quietly connected. That is the bridge in daily use: two banks, one river, one life.
