A bridge exists to be walked, and Journey is the key of the crossing itself. It teaches that the distance between where you are and where you long to be is covered one honest step at a time, and that the walking changes you more than the arriving does. Setbacks, rests, and wrong turns are all part of the path, not departures from it.

Journey asks you to travel light, to notice where you actually are rather than where you think you should be, and to trust that a path walked with attention is never wasted, however winding it looks from above.
