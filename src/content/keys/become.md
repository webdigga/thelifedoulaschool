Become is not about turning into someone new. It is the patient work of releasing everything you were told to be, so the person underneath can finally breathe. Old roles, old stories, and old survival habits served their purpose; this key thanks them and sets them down.

Becoming happens on both sides of the bridge at once: the human side, where choices, boundaries, and daily habits slowly reshape a life, and the spiritual side, where you remember that you were never broken to begin with. You are not learning anything new; you are remembering.
