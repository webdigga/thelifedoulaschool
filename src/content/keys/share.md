---
title: Share
order: 8
essence: Passing the light along, for others and for the world unseen.
description: 'Share is the eighth Bridging Key: passing on what you have learned with open hands, for self and others, seen and unseen, never pushing.'
sevenWays: []
---

A bridge is built to be crossed by more than one person. Share is the final key: what you have learned, healed, and become is not meant to be stored but passed along, gently and generously, to whoever is ready for it. Teaching and learning are two directions of the same crossing.

Sharing on this path is done with open hands. It never pushes, promotes, judges, or forces; it offers. Sometimes it looks like teaching, sometimes like listening, and sometimes like simply living in a way that leaves a light on for the next traveller.
