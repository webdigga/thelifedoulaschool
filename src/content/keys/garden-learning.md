A garden is never finished and never in a hurry, and that is exactly how this key asks you to learn. Garden Learning replaces cramming and striving with planting and tending: small seeds of practice, watered a little each day, given light and time. Some things flower quickly; others need a whole season underground first. Both are growth.

This key also teaches honest weeding: noticing the habits and stories that crowd out what you are trying to grow, and removing them gently, roots and all. What you tend, you become.
